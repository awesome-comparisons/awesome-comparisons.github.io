Awesome Comparisons is a curated collection of side-by-side comparisons
for tools, frameworks, and services that solve similar problems.

## What's covered

- **Languages** — history, philosophy, syntax, and semantics across
  JavaScript, TypeScript, Python, Go, Rust, Java, Kotlin, Swift, C, C++,
  C#, Ruby, PHP, and Elixir
- **Frameworks** — frontend, mobile, desktop, and backend frameworks
- **Misc** — databases, web/application servers, and git hosting
  platforms

## Why comparisons?

Picking the right tool is hard when every landing page claims to be the
best option. Instead of marketing copy, this project focuses on:

- **Objective criteria** — performance, bundle size, ecosystem, learning curve
- **Real trade-offs** — no tool wins at everything
- **Up-to-date data** — comparisons are revisited as tools evolve

> Good comparisons don't tell you what to pick. They tell you what you're
> trading away either way.

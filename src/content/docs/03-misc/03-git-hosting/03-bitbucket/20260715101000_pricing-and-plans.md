---
title: Pricing and Plans
description: How Bitbucket structures its pricing and plans.
---

Bitbucket Cloud offers a Free plan for small teams with a limited number of users and a modest allotment of Pipelines build minutes, then scales up through Standard and Premium plans that increase user limits, add merge checks and required approvals, and expand CI/CD minutes and security controls. Pricing is generally structured per user per month, with plan tiers primarily differing in collaboration and governance features rather than storage.

For organizations that need on-premises deployment, Atlassian offers Bitbucket Data Center (the successor to Bitbucket Server), which is licensed separately from the cloud plans and is aimed at larger enterprises needing high availability, dedicated infrastructure, and integration with self-hosted Jira and Confluence instances.
